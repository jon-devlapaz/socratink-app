# Generation Before Recognition

## Principle
Socratink must require the learner to generate before exposing answer-shaped recognition aids.

## Evidence
This doctrine is reflected in [Thermostat Drill Logs](../sources/drill-chat-log-thermostat-session-1.md).

## Product Implication
UI and retrieval flows should avoid answer leakage and treat cold attempts as unscored generation.
