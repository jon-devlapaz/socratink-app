# Spacing Needs Interleaving

## Pattern
Time alone is an incomplete guard; interleaving supports more truthful spacing behavior.

## Evidence
The mechanism and drill-derived evidence jointly suggest that spacing should not collapse into a raw timer.

## Inference
Interleaving likely carries the real cognitive weight, while the timer is only a coarse proxy.

## Product Implication
Eligibility logic and copy should keep interleaving explicit instead of teaching the learner to wait passively.
